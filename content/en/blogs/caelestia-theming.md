Ok so you have Caelestia... but you can have more!
Caelestia in your browser, Caelestia in your sddm, Caelestia in your IDE, Caelestia in your Minecraft, CAELESTIA EVERYWHERE GNAHAHAHAHAHAH!!!

## Integrated themes
All the apps that have themes that come with Caelestia when you download it:
- Thunar
- btop
- Firefox based browsers
- Chromium based browsers
- Papirus folder icons
- Zed
- Spicetify
- Pandora Launcher
- VS Code / codium
